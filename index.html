--- layout: application title: HOOYAH Paintball ---

Hooyah Paintball, situated in Ruimsig on the West Rand, offers a safe and fun paintball range, catering for groups of all sizes. We offer two man- made fields that can be opened to form one extra-large field.

Hooyah Paintball uses semi-automatic high quality BT rental markers (guns) and quality masks with double thermal lenses for fog free night games.

We have a paved covered seating area, tuck shop, clean ablutions and safe parking.

Hooyah Paintball is an alcohol free venue.
